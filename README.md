
## 🎥 Video Demonstrations

We provide video demonstrations of the proposed **STUD** framework and its ablated variants in two typical **Out-of-Distribution (OOD) driving scenarios** for unsignalized intersection navigation.

### Abbreviations
- **STUD**: Full framework (Safe Trajectory Optimization with Uncertainty-aware CBF)
- **STUD w/o SF**: STUD without Safety Filter
- **STUD w/o UA**: STUD without Uncertainty Awareness
